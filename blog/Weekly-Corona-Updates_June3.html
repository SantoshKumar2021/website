World & India Weekly Updates Coronavirus ☠[2020]

This is a weekly update on Coronavirus stats of India and rest of the world. Every Wednesday the blog is updated showcasing the stats of the past week (Tuesday to Monday). We focus mostly on weekly coronavirus stats both in India and rest of the world. There are plenty of online sites that do daily stats our focus will be to compare week on week progress and which countries are doing well and which countries are struggling by ranking them based on numbers reported in a given week. Additionally we also share a weekly forecast or prediction of next weeks cases for India based on data patterns. Objective is to create awarness/factcheck based on data rather than misinterpreted news broadcasts.

June 3rd Update - Data period 26th May to 1st June

Last Week Forecast Result:

We forecasted as per best/worst case upto 6.5k cases per day and hinted 7.5k average daily cases possibility and we have recorded about 7.6k daily average cases for last week. Our best/worst case scenario last week was to reach ~1.82-1.84lakh by Sunday which was reached by Saturday itself. Predicted upto 2.05lakh by Wednesday and it got reached a day ahead on Tuesday itself.
